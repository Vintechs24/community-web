Explore Our Services

Gym

Our fully equipped gym offers a variety of equipment for fitness enthusiasts. It’s open 7 days a week, providing you with the flexibility to achieve your fitness goals at your own pace.

  • Hours: 6 AM - 9 PM
  • Available Equipment: Treadmills, Free Weights, Resistance Machines, etc.
  • Membership Options: Daily, Weekly, Monthly Memberships available.

Swimming Pool

Our swimming pool is the perfect place to cool down or get a great workout. It’s open daily with morning and evening sessions to accommodate your schedule.

  • Hours: 7 AM - 7 PM
  • Facilities: Olympic size pool, Heated pool, Poolside Lounge
  • Programs: Swimming lessons, Open swim, Competitive swim

Yoga Classes

Our yoga classes cater to all levels of experience. Whether you’re a beginner or advanced, our certified instructors will guide you through sessions that promote health, flexibility, and relaxation.

  • Hours: 8 AM - 10 AM, 5 PM - 7 PM
  • Class Types: Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa, Restorative Yoga
  • Additional Info: Mat and props provided
